Nodes pass messages to their neighbors. Relationships are encoded as edges with scalar weights. Edges are passive conduits — they don't compute, they just carry signal from node to node.

DELTA promotes edges to first-class citizens. Nodes and edges both carry rich representations and attend to each other simultaneously in parallel. Edge-to-edge attention enables reasoning about relationships between relationships — the key to compositional inference.
